About us

We believe everyone deserves the unconditional love of a pet and at Snouters, our mission is to make it easier to experience that love. Our mission is to simplify all aspects of being a pet parent.

Tech Driven

Snouters is a technology platform that supports pet boarding, dog walking, veterinary care, and dog training services across India. We empower our community of trusted pet boarders, groomers and walkers to run their own pet care businesses on Snouters by providing them with the right tools.

People who love pets

Whether you need a dog walker for the day, overnight pet boarding for a month, or dog day care, there’s a Snouter who’s the perfect match for you, your pets, and your lifestyle.

MICHBIZ


MICHBIZ is India’s largest network of 5-star pet home stays. Snouters connects pet parents with loving pet care providers who offer overnight services, including boarding and in-home pet sitting, as well as daytime services, including doggy daycare, dog walking, drop-in visits, and grooming.
